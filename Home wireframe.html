NutritionIT This is NutritionIT Welcome to NutritionIT. Here is where you take control of your nutrition. By tracking your calories and your food throughout the day you become one step closer to meeting your fitness goal. Here's how it works:
  • Enter a food you have eaten into the diary for your breakfast, lunch, dinner, or a snack
  • The diary will then add this to a list and display its calories
  • Return to the calorie screen to see what you have consumed throughout the day
