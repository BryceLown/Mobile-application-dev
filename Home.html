NutritionIT This is NutritionIT Welcome to NutritionIT. Here is where you take control of your nutrition. By tracking your calories and your food throughout the day you become one step closer to meeting your fitness goals. Here's how it works:
  • Search for a food you've eaten in your diary
  • This will then tell you its nutritional information such as calories, carbohydrates, protein, and fat
  • Once you have learned the nutritional value you can add these together in the calories page to calculate the total calories from all that you have eaten
